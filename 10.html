Visit San Juan Bautista:
The City of History

Recommendations for a day trip to this cute California town!

  • Historic must sees:
    • Mission San Juan Bautista
    • Casa Zanetta
    • Plaza Hotel
    • Castro-Breen Adobe

  • Good Eats and Drinks:
    • Jardines Restaurant
    • Hapa Bros
    • Brewery Twenty Five
    • 18th Barrel Tasting Room

  • Things to do:
    • See a play at El Teatro Campesino
    • Hike at Fremont's Peak
    • Go antique shopping
    • View art galleries
    • Buy art from Artsy Chica Boutique
