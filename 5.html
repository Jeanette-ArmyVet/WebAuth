San Juan Bautista

Recommendations for a day trip in this cute little town!

  1. Historic must sees:
    • Mission San Juan Bautista
    • Casa Zanetta
    • Plaza Hotel
  2. Good Eats and Drinks:
    • Jardines Restaurant
    • Hapa Bros
    • Brewery Twenty Five
    • 18th Barrel Tasting Room
  3. Things to do:
    • See a play at El Teatro Campesino
    • Hike at Fremont's Peak
    • Go antique shopping
    • View art galleries
    • Buy art at Artsy Chica Boutique
